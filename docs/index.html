Need4Viz is a French-American software company founded in 2020 that develops N4V FOR WEBI, the only SAP-certified extension suite providing 60+ interactive graphical components natively integrated into SAP BusinessObjects Web Intelligence (Webi).

The solution transforms standard Webi reports into modern, interactive dashboards with advanced charts, full GIS mapping, HTML5 export capabilities, and AI-powered analytics. It operates as a native add-on — meaning organizations can modernize their SAP BI environment without changing tools, without writing code, and without any change management effort.

Need4Viz serves over 150,000 users across 80+ organizations worldwide, from the French Gendarmerie Nationale and Banque de France to the American Red Cross and McKesson (Fortune 8). The company is headquartered in Paris with a US office in Miami.

"Need4Viz provides the type of functionality that Web Intelligence users have been asking SAP for years. The team is responsive and a pleasure to deal with." — Hayden Gill, Associate Director BI, University of Queensland

Products & Modules

The N4V FOR WEBI platform consists of four complementary modules:

N4V Widgets

60+ interactive, drillable chart components built natively into SAP Web Intelligence. Includes Gantt charts, Waterfall, Pivot Table, KPI Tiles, Timelines, Multichart, Chart Tile, Drillbar navigation, and more. All widgets support drill-down, alerters, and responsive layouts.

N4V Maps

Full Geographic Information System (GIS) integrated into Webi. Supports Google Maps, OpenStreetMap, and offline maps. Create drillable territory maps, custom floor plans, WebMapLine routes, and business-ready geo dashboards directly inside your reports.

N4V Publisher

Export interactive HTML5 dashboards that can be viewed in any web browser — without requiring SAP BusinessObjects licenses. Share reports with clients, partners, and external collaborators. Also supports export to Word, Excel, PowerPoint, PDF, PNG, and CSV.

N4V Intelligence

AI-powered analytics module with three capabilities: Natural Language Query (NLQ) to ask questions in plain language, Chart Recommender for automatic visualization suggestions, and Predictive Series for trend detection and forecasting.

AI & Intelligence

N4V Intelligence brings artificial intelligence directly into SAP Web Intelligence, enabling users to interact with their data using natural language and receive intelligent recommendations.

Natural Language Query (NLQ)

Ask questions in plain language (English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese) and receive instant Webi queries and visualizations. Requires SAP BO 4.3 SP03+.

Chart Recommender

AI analyzes your data structure and automatically suggests the most relevant chart type. Eliminates guesswork in data visualization. Requires SAP BO 4.3 SP04+.

Predictive Series

Automatic trend detection and time-series forecasting built into Webi charts. Identify patterns and predict future values without external tools.

SAP Partnership

Need4Viz is an SAP Silver Partner, and N4V FOR WEBI is officially available on the SAP Store and AWS Marketplace.

At the SAP Analytics Roadmap 2025, SAP confirmed that Web Intelligence remains a pillar of their analytics strategy. SAP explicitly named Need4Viz as the first example of their partner strategy, acknowledging that the BusinessObjects SDK enables partners to deliver visualization and AI capabilities that SAP itself does not build natively.

"SAP doesn't invest in widgets, maps, and AI because Need4Viz already does a fantastic job." — Eric Fenollosa, SAP

SAP Freemium Integration

Starting with SAP BusinessObjects BI 4.3 SP03, SAP bundles 7 Need4Viz chart components as a freemium offer, allowing users to evaluate N4V capabilities at no cost. A dedicated N4V Freemium version is also available.

SAP BI 2025 Compatibility

N4V FOR WEBI is fully compatible with SAP BI 2025 (the successor to SAP BusinessObjects BI). For details on the SAP BI 2025 release, see: SAP BusinessObjects BI 2025: all the new features.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Need4Viz?

Need4Viz is the only software vendor providing 60+ interactive graphical components natively integrated into SAP BusinessObjects Web Intelligence (Webi). The N4V FOR WEBI extension transforms standard Webi reports into modern, interactive dashboards with advanced charts, GIS mapping, HTML5 export, and AI-powered analytics.

Is Need4Viz compatible with SAP BI 2025?

Yes. N4V FOR WEBI is fully compatible with SAP BI 2025, SAP BO 4.3, and SAP BO 4.2 SP08+.

How does Need4Viz compare to Power BI?

Unlike Power BI, which requires migrating to a completely new platform, Need4Viz integrates natively into your existing SAP Web Intelligence environment. You keep your universes, queries, and reports while gaining modern visualization, mapping, and AI capabilities. There is no change management, no data migration, and no additional licensing model.

Can I share dashboards without SAP BusinessObjects licenses?

Yes. With N4V Publisher, you can export interactive HTML5 dashboards viewable in any web browser, without requiring SAP BO licenses. This enables unlimited sharing with clients, partners, and external collaborators.

What AI features does Need4Viz offer?

N4V Intelligence provides Natural Language Query (NLQ) to ask questions in plain language, Chart Recommender for automatic visualization suggestions, and Predictive Series for trend detection. Available from SAP BO 4.3 SP03+.

Does Need4Viz require coding?

No. N4V FOR WEBI is a code-free solution. All widgets, maps, and AI features are configured through a visual interface directly within SAP Web Intelligence.

Is there a free version?

Yes. Need4Viz offers a Freemium version for SAP BO 4.3 SP02+. SAP also bundles 7 N4V charts with SAP BO 4.3 SP03 as a freemium offer.
